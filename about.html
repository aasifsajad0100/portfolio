About

A bit about who I am, what I work on, and where I'm headed.

I'm a 3rd-year BSc Data Science student at the Islamic University of Science and Technology, with a solid hold on HTML, C, C++, Python, R, and MS Office — comfortable moving between low-level programming fundamentals and higher-level data workflows like cleaning, exploring, and modeling data.

I completed an internship at NIT Srinagar, where I worked hands-on with machine learning using Python — from raw, messy data through preprocessing and exploratory analysis, to building and evaluating models whose results actually meant something.

Data science, to me, sits at the intersection of statistics, code, and curiosity: I like the process of turning a spreadsheet full of numbers into a model that can actually predict something useful. Outside of coursework, I spend time in R for statistical exploration and Python for applied ML, and I'm steadily picking up the surrounding tooling — Git, SQL, and data visualization — that turns a model into something shippable.

I'm looking to grow into a Data / ML Engineering role — an internship or entry-level position where I can keep building pipelines and models that hold up outside a notebook, while learning fast from people further along the path. I'm also open to freelance website-building work using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — so feel free to reach out if that's what you need.
